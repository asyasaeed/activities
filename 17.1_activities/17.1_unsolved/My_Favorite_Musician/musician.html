Freddie Mercury

Singer, Songwriter, Record Producer, and Lead vocalist of the rock band Queen


Born: September 5, 1946, Stone Town, Tanzania
Died: November 24, 1991, Kensington
Full name: Farrokh Bulsara
Nationality: British

Regarded as one of the greatest singers in the history of rock music, he was known for his
flamboyant stage persona and four-octave vocal range. Mercury defied the conventions of a
rock frontman, with his highly theatrical style influencing the artistic direction of Queen.
